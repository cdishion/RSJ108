How to make an awesome RSJ Bleach Tie-Dye Shirt

Assembly time: 30 minutes
Awesome rating: 40% awesome

Are you wondering how to turn a boring T-shirt into a cool design? It's really fun to make. Why don't you try to make it yourself? Follow the steps and you'll make a cool T-shirt!

  • Things You'll Need

  • Bleach
  • Water
  • Rubber Gloves and Rubber Bands
  • Spray Bottle
  • Solid Colored T-shirt
  • Laundry Detergent

  1. Find proper workspace in well ventilated area.

    Bleach is strong so either work outside or in a space with a window or fan.

  2. Pick out dark cotton T-shirt

    The darker the T-shirt, the better and more vibrant the results will be.

    This is an example of a cotton T-shirt that will work.

  3. Using rubber bands, twist T-shirt into desired pattern design.

    The part of the T-shirt the rubber bands cover will maintain T-shirt's original color. Pattern designs include spirals, knots, and twirls.

  4. In the spray bottle, mix 1/2 bleach and 1/2 water.

    Spray mixture onto T-shirt. You can use as much or as little bleach solution as desired.

  5. Allow T-shirt to sit for 8-10 minutes with the sprayed bleach.

    After soaking for 8-10 minutes, wash T-shirt mild detergent and then place in dryer or hang to dry.
